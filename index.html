Don't Know What To Eat?

So it's time to eat, but you don't know what to eat. You try searching for restaurants, but it takes so much time and effort. Isn't there be an easier way to discover good food?

We're building the solution.

Our Features

Personalized Food Recommendations

Zeek gives you personalized food recommendations based on your preferences. By posting recommendations and interacting with the app, Zeek learns what you like and gives you better recommendations.

The Food Social Network

Connect with other food lovers and share the great food you've had with the world! Instead of reviews, you share food recommendations, just like how you would recommend a place to a friend.

Save Your Favourites

Like a recommendation you found on Zeek? Save it to your favorites! You can refer to them anytime you want, and Zeek will even remind you to try them!
